About me

Hi! I am Burhan Noman, a User Experience Engineer. I blend knowledge and passion to come up with intuitive and beautifully crafted User Experience. I have a strong empathy with users and never ending love for design and technology. While designing, I embrace ambiguity, I believe in iterations and I think of failures as a design experiments with the opportunity to learn.

Designer + Engineer

I completed my Bachelors of Engineering in Computer & Information Systems Engineering from NED University, Pakistan. After playing a vital role in first unicorn of Pakistan, Careem and helping Aurea with its customer experience platforms, i decided to purse my education in Interactive Design. Currently, i am enjoying roller coaster ride of my masters in field of Interactive Design from Savannah College of Art and Design, USA.
