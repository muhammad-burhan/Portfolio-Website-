River Street Savannah is the Re-design of River Street Website. River Street is a historic street of Savannah which has a lot of historic and artistic importance. The street is alongside with the port of Savannah. People from all over the world enjoy their time in Savannah by visiting River-street and its attractions.
Participation: Team Lead, UX Designer, Developer & Photography

Design Process

Live Interviews

To get better understanding of the user's need and the visitor's preference we conducted on-site interviews. We talked to shop owners and visitors including couples and families.

Design Thinking
