The idea is to create a futuristic complex UI for the advance real-time critical systems. I took inspirations from different games like Metal Gear solid V and took references from the designer - Ashthrop. I blended still images, videos and complex figures to come up with the “Search -> Select ->Target -> Destroy” for the missile launching critical system.
