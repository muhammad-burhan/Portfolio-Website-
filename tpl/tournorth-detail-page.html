Goals

Project goals: Design and develop a responsive storytelling website.
Personal goal for the project: Being a Pakistani, I care about the perception of international community towards Pakistan. And when there are hundreds of negative things floating around, I want to show the positive image of Pakistan and tell other communities what this country has to offer. I have been travelling to different parts of the country and want to leverage my journeys to take viewers into the beautiful valleys and introduce the most humble and friendliest people on planet.

Solution

Tour North is a storytelling website demonstrating the journey to the North of Pakistan - Gilgit. Sounds, videos and images takes user to the amazing valleys and creates a unique audio-visual experience to develop a narrative story environment.

Individual Project

Sketches, Wireframes, Mock-ups, Web Development, Photography

Sketches

The first thing I did was seared for tons of inspirations around the web, looked different layouts and get inspirations from those websites. After that I realized that the content is the most important factor in storytelling, so I start writing my content and sort images, videos and sound content I intend to display. Soon after that I start sketching the layout and ideas.

Sound + Visuals

To create an experience and create a feeling of serenity I used slow instrumental sounds with the blend of actual sound of the nature, lakes & valleys.

Micro Interactions

While designing I also emphasized on creating micro interactions. This makes web elements looks real as they have a life in them and responds back to user as they interact with elements.

Going with hamburger navigation

Initially I decided to go with the top fixed navigation, but fixed navigation was always coming in the way of 100% width content I intend to display. I also realize that since the website is one page and there is no unusual lengthy scroll I can bundle the navigation into hamburger icon. But I believe there is still a lot of opportunities for improvement.

Wireframes

After making above decisions i quickly came up with the basic wireframes to inform Hi-fi mock-ups decisions.

Color Palette & Font

As far as visual design is concerned i decided to go with one color only and rest of the colors will be brought by the content itself.

Development

The website was developed using basic front-end technologies like HTML5, SASS, JavaScript, JQuery and Bootstrap framework. The website is responsive and use serve side caching to load resources quickly.
