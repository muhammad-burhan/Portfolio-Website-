TOUR NORTH

Gilgit Pakistan

The idea is to tell a story of a journey to the North of Pakistan - Gilgit. Sounds, videos and images takes user to the amazing valleys and creates a unique audio visual experience to develop a narrative story environment.

The website is a single page responsive website and is designed and developed using Adobe creative suite, Just in mind prototyper, HTML5, SASS, JS & JQuery.
